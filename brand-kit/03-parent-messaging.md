# Parent Messaging

Status: draft seed

## WhatsApp Updates

- Short enough to read quickly.
- Concrete bullet points are preferred.
- Include logistics clearly when relevant: food, location, timing, tomorrow's plan.
- Preserve the feeling of the day without inventing details.

## Email Updates

- Use for details that need permanence or clarity.
- Keep the tone personal and calm.
- Do not make parents dig for next steps.
